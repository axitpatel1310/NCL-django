Our Mission

Our mission is to bring together cricket enthusiasts from all walks of life, promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and a love for the game. The Nvpas Cricket League (NCL) aims to provide a platform for emerging talent while fostering community spirit through the sport we all cherish

Our Journey

The NCL began with a passion for cricket and a vision to create a tournament where players could showcase their skills and compete at a high level. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated local cricket events, attracting teams and fans from across the region

Our Values

At NCL, we believe in fair play, integrity, and excellence. We are committed to maintaining a competitive yet respectful environment where every player and team has the opportunity to shine and inspire others

Our Future

As we look ahead, NCL aims to expand its reach, inviting even more teams and fans to participate in this exciting cricket journey. We’re dedicated to nurturing future stars of the sport and creating unforgettable memories for everyone involved.
